‘Kelab Kewangan Pelajar’ or Students’ Financial Club provides a platform for schools to organize financial education activities as part of the school co-curriculum activities. Club members can engage in money and finance related workshops, quizzes, contests, games, knowledge-sharing sessions and enjoy visits to financial service providers.

Teachers may refer to these guides and may adopt or modify the activities based on the students’ needs and level of maturity
